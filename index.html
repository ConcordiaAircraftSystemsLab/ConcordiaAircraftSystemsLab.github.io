Our Mission

Our mission is to develop design methodologies and tools for the next generation of environmentally friendly aircraft. With these methods and tools, we want to enable a faster integration of new technologies into future aircraft.

We focus on system and subsystems considerations and how they can be included in the conceptual design phase to establish concept feasibility and more mature requirements.
